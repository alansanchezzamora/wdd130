    We have worked with our clients in the development of Corporate Strategy, Business Strategy, and Functional Strategy. We ensure the presence of the following elements:

  • Strategy is a deliberate search for a plan of action that will develop a business’s competitive advantage and compound it. For any company the search is an iterative process that begins with a recognition of where you are and what you have now. — Bruce Henderson
  • Operational effectiveness (OE) means performing similar activities better than rivals perform them. In contrast, strategic positioning means performing different activities from rivals’ or performing similar activities in different ways — Michael E. Porter

    We have helped our clients explore, identify, and seize new business opportunities. In order to develop a new business, it is imperative to understand and define:

  • Business Strategy: The market’s potential, the firm’s value proposition, and the necessary resources
  • Corporate governance: The rules governing the board, its make-up, shareholding, and capital contributions
  • Deal Structuring: Understanding the value that each partner contributes, valuing businesses and setting relevant milestones to complete each transaction.
  • Entry Strategy: Analyzing alternatives to entering a given market alone, in partnership, or via acquisitions, evaluating candidates for acquisition.
